# BR Pest Control — Product Context

## Product Shape
This is a lead-generation website for a local sole trader, not a broad brochure site. The product succeeds when a visitor quickly trusts Ben and either calls or submits an enquiry.

## Audience
Primary visitors are homeowners, landlords, farm owners, and small business operators in and around Ripon, Boroughbridge, Thirsk, and Bedale who have an active pest problem.

Most visitors are likely on mobile and in a hurry. The site should answer: "Can Ben handle this, does he cover my area, and how do I contact him now?"

## Conversion Priorities
1. Click-to-call visible above the fold and persistent on mobile.
2. Contact form simple enough to complete under stress.
3. Credentials visible without making the tone corporate.
4. Coverage and local relevance repeated naturally across the site.

## SEO Priorities
- Dedicated area pages for Ripon, Boroughbridge, Thirsk, and Bedale.
- Wasp nest removal receives extra emphasis because it is the strongest evidenced service and review theme.
- Page titles and meta descriptions must be town/service specific.
- Internal links should connect home, services, wasp removal, and area pages.

## Tone
Use UK English. Write in a local, approachable, practical tone. Avoid inflated claims, invented guarantees, or national-brand language.

Preferred messaging:
- "Professional, reliable & efficient"
- "Fully insured"
- "Here to help with all your pest problems, no matter how large or small"
- Fast response, especially for wasp nests

## Trust Signals
- RSPH Level 2 Award in Pest Management
- BPCA member
- Fully insured
- Five-star review theme
- Ben is a real local operator, not a faceless national call centre

## Known Launch Gaps
- Exact remaining review copy.
- Whether to show full names or first names only.
- Final logo asset.
- WhatsApp click-to-chat decision.
- Opening hours or public response-time promise.
- Ben-owned photos.
