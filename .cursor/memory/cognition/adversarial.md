# Adversarial — Red-Team Protocol

Attack the preferred solution before implementation.

## SEO Attacks
- Are area pages too similar?
- Does each page have a unique title and description?
- Are service and town keywords used naturally?
- Is the site reinforcing GBP NAP consistently?

## Conversion Attacks
- Is the phone number visible without scrolling on mobile?
- Is the enquiry form too long or unclear?
- Is the primary CTA buried behind decorative content?
- Are success and failure states obvious?

## Trust And Content Attacks
- Does copy invent proof, guarantees, services, or review text?
- Are credentials stated accurately?
- Is Ben presented as local and approachable?

## Privacy And Security Attacks
- Is the residential address exposed anywhere?
- Are secrets kept server-side?
- Does the honeypot avoid revealing spam rules?

## Accessibility Attacks
- Are labels explicit?
- Is focus state visible?
- Does the red palette maintain contrast?
- Is heading order logical?

## Output
Before large work, identify the top risks and the mitigation. After work, verify the mitigations were applied.
